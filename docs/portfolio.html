EDITING THE PORTFOLIO SECTION

To edit the portfolio section of our theme (Only one will be shown);

<div id="music" class="ichigo-h1"><ion-icon name="musical-notes-outline"></ion-icon> New Music</div> <div class="container"> <div class="projectable"><img src="./systems/images/kei.png" /><br /><h3>Kei</h3> <a href="#" class="listen-button">Listen</a></div> </div> </div>

Note: The "projectable" div corresponds to one project. The <img src="..." /> contains the image of the project. The link is found within the <a href="..." />, and your text is placed outside of it.
